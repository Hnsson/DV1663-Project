About Our Project

Welcome to our social media web application! Our platform is designed to simplify the online experience for students at BTH by providing a seamless Single Sign-On (SSO) solution. With our application, students can easily access and interact with a variety of features, including posting, managing discussions, and staying informed about what happens around campus.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a vibrant online community where students can connect, share, and engage with each other effortlessly. By eliminating the hassle of managing multiple login credentials, we aim to make it easier for students to stay connected and informed about what's happening at BTH.

Why Choose Us?

  • Simplified login process through SSO integration
  • Robust features for posting, discussion management
  • Anonymous discussion options for open dialogue
  • User-friendly interface designed with students in mind

Join us in building a thriving online community at BTH!
